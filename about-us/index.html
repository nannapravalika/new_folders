About us

Aviacons was established in the year 2007 and incorporated as Private Limited Company in the year 2012, with a view to impart world class aviation training programs to Professional Pilots, Student Pilots and Aviation Industry enthusiasts. Aviacons is backed by most experienced professionals in Pilot Training, Theoretical Training, Business & Finance Operations in Airline & GA Sector. Aviacons has helped more than 1500 aspiring pilots in their incredible journey from Classroom to Cockpit.

Aviacons Advantages


Most Affordable Ground School in South India. Start your Pilot training with just 2 Lakhs.

Exclusive Ground School Program Expanding Avenues for Pilot Training in India and Abroad.

Highly Experienced and Devoted Faculty for 100% result in DGCA CPL Examintions.

Interactive Sessions and Guest Lectures by Airline Pilots and Air Force Veterans.

VISION


To strengthen and progressively attain the highest standards in aviation training. To expand the professional horizons for creating an effective and resourceful workforce for serving the industry on the ground and in the air.

MISSION


To introduce an array of industry-specific courses and impart quality training in-house as well as with collaborative arrangements with reputed institutions abroad. Focus on providing skilled and knowledgeable workforce for meeting the growing industry demand. These courses shall be career oriented, in line with best of industry norms & standards from across the globe, and at the same time cost effective.
